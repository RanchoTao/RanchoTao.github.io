**BIMSA首届 Mathematics & Artificial Intelligence 本科生.**

I am an undergraduate student in Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence at [BIMSA](https://www.bimsa.cn/).

I am interested in learning theory, quantitative systems, and AI-assisted workflows. I build small systems to study how people make decisions, manage attention, and learn over long time horizons.

My current focus is to strengthen my mathematical foundation, implement useful systems, and gradually move toward research problems where theory and real-world use meet.

## Current direction

I am trying to become a research-builder: someone who studies foundations, builds prototypes, and uses implementation details to sharpen research taste.

* **Mathematics and learning theory** — probability, optimization, reinforcement learning, and generalization.
* **Quantitative systems** — trading research workflows, data pipelines, backtesting, and uncertainty-aware evaluation.
* **Systems for thought** — tools that help people externalize attention, preserve context, and learn over long horizons.
